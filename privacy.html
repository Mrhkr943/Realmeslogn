Privacy Policy for gmailX

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Data We Access

gmailX accesses your Gmail messages only to display them to you in the dashboard. We access:

  • Email subject lines
  • Sender information
  • Email content (to show you your messages)

How We Use Data

Your email data is used solely to provide the email dashboard functionality. We do not:

  • Store your emails on our servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for any purpose other than displaying your inbox

Data Storage

All data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored. Session data is temporary and deleted when you log out.
